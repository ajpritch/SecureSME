About

Secure SME is a framework designed to help guide and inform decisions made by small to medium enterprises in the united kingdom.

The Framework

This framework was created by Alex, a student of the University of South Wales (USW) to fulfil part of the criteria for the Cyber Dissertation module of the Computer Security BSc (Hons) degree.

The framework is designed to fill the gap in knowledge between small to medium enterprises, including startups, sole traders, and business partners with what is required of them by law. The framework provides general advice and guidance, including staff training, for free in a verity of privacy and security related topics.

The Author

For years, Alex has been passionate about cybersecurity and digital rights. In 2022, he took a significant step towards becoming an IT professional by enrolling in a full-time program at USW to study cybersecurity. He strongly believes that many data breaches occurring in small to medium enterprises are preventable. Rather than creating something that few will see or directly benefit from, Alex has developed and published this online framework at his own expense, aiming to provide valuable resources for everyone who needs it.
